About me

Hi! My name is Melany Chang Mok. I was born in the United States of America, but raised in Venezuela. Growing up, I always had a passion for art. I was not poor, however, I could not join any academy, so I would practice by myself because I felt at peace. Up to now, I still feel the same about art. I'm always distracted looking at everything in great detail trying to understand it. Thus, I feel like this is the reason why I did not pursue any other carrier. Not to mention that even if I wanted to do so, I wouldn't be able to do it because art is always in my mind. Currently I'm studying to become an artist at Borough of Manhattan Community College.

If you will like to contact me, you can do it at melany.changmok@stu.bmcc.cuny.edu
